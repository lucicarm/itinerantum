The travellers

We go where
curiosity leads

Itinerantum is a travel blog built on a simple belief: the best way to understand the world is to move through it slowly, with open eyes and an open mind.

We document our journeys not just to share where we have been, but to show how travel transforms perspective — one country, one meal, one sunrise at a time.

Philosophy

Independent,
always

We organise our own trips wherever possible — it gives us a freedom that no guided tour can replicate. Every story here is written from first-hand experience, with practical tips for travellers who want to do the same.

From bullet trains in China to 4×4 tracks in the Masai Mara, we believe every journey deserves to be done properly.
