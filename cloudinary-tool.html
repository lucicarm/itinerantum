This tool runs entirely in your browser. Your credentials are never sent anywhere except directly to Cloudinary.

Step 1 — Enter your Cloudinary credentials

This will fetch the names of all your uploaded photos. It may take a moment if you have over 1,000 assets.

Step 2 — Match photos to posts

The tool has tried to automatically match your Cloudinary photo names to the original WordPress filenames. Green = matched automatically. Orange = best guess, please verify. Red = no match found, please select manually using the dropdown.
